--lambda LAMBDA
[-p PASSES]
[-c CHECKPOINT]
Optional arguments are in square brackets.
Examples:
./cdt --spherical -n 32000 -t 11 --alpha 0.6 -k 1.1 --lambda 0.1 --passes 1000
