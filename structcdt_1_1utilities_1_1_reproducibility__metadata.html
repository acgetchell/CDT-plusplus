Detailed Description

Provenance recorded next to every stochastic triangulation.

Checkpoints are deliberately snapshots rather than resumable simulation states: the payload does not serialize mutable RNG state. Payload-derived counts, time bounds, and fingerprints are reconciled with the serialized triangulation before publication. Callers remain responsible for supplying truthful run configuration and RNG provenance.
