Detailed Description

template<typename ManifoldType>
requires (ManifoldType::dimension == 3)
class MoveStrategy< Strategies::METROPOLIS, ManifoldType >

Metropolis-Hastings algorithm strategy.

The Metropolis-Hastings algorithm is a Markov Chain Monte Carlo method. For target weight \(\pi(T)\propto e^{-S(T)}\), a proposal from triangulation \(T\) to \(T'\) is accepted with probability:

\[\min\left(1, e^{S(T)-S(T')} \frac{q(T\mid T')}{q(T'\mid T)}\right).\]

The proposal-ratio construction follows the Hastings transition rule; see Metropolis-Hastings algorithmMetropolis-Hastings algorithm.
